ALAN TURING






  • Alan Mathison Turing was born in London on June 23, 1912,
    in a family which had worked in India.
  • Since he was 6, Turing realized he was very interested in mathematical and scientific subjects, and he wanted to attend a scientific high school, but he had to attended classical studies where he had great difficulty with Latin and literary subjects.
  • After the diploma, in 1931, he attended the King's College University of Cambridge and in 1934, he graduated in Quantistic mechanics, Logic and Theory of probability with full marks, and in 1936 he studied at Princeton University, where he took his second degree in Logic, Algebra and Theory of numbers.
